As seen in the yearly incidence maps of Chicago crimes from 2016-2019 below, the pattern of arrests does not vary greatly. As of May 2, 2020, there were 266 398 recorded crimes in Chicago in 2016 with 52 792 arrests, 261 398 crimes with 51 649 arrests in 2017, 260 105 crimes with 52 252 arrests in 2018, and 256 908 crimes with 54 956 arrests in 2019. Arrests occurred for roughly 20% of recorded crimes in 2016, 2017 and 2018 and 21% in 2019.
