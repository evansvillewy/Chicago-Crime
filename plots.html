Visualization overview
Pie Chart:
As seen in the pie chart and the crime map page, most crimes in Chicago go unsolved. There was little change in the arrest rate year over year. The years 2018 and 2019 showed slight increases in arrests.
Bar 1:
Of those crimes for which an arrest was made, most were non-violent crimes. Crimes such as gambling, prostitution, public indecency, and narcotics had nearly a 100% arrest rate. This was consistent in the years examined.
Bar 2:
For the top ten crimes by incidence, all except narcotics crimes have an arrest percentage less than 25%. Again, this is consistent year to year.
