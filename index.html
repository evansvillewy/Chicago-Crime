Most violent and property crimes in Chicago go unsolved.

Crime data from the Chicago Data Portal (https://data.cityofchicago.org) for 2016-2019 has been analyzed to visualize this reality. This site provides insights into the data to clearly show the crimes that go unsolved in the Chicago area.

We place the crimes on interactive maps utilizing Tableau and highlight where crimes get solved and go unsolved.

Finally, through the use of Machine Learning, we compare the results various methods of training computer models to predict whether an unsolved crime will be solved.
